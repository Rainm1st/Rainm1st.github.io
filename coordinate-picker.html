🏋️ Gym Map Coordinate Picker

Click on your gym floor plan to capture x/y positions for each equipment piece.

How to use:
1. Click Load Gym Map and select your floor plan image (JPG/PNG).
2. Click directly on each equipment location on the map — the crosshair snaps to click position.
3. Pick the equipment name from the dropdown, then click Add Point.
4. When done, copy the Export Code and paste it into gym-locations.js.
